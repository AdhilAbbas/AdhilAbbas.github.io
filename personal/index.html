TCP/IP Connections

Its the protocol that is used to transfer data between two different computers.
Main advantage of TCP protocol is that it will ensure the sented data is not corrupted or lost during transmission.
TCP is connection oriented ,that is it establishes a connection through a three-way-handshake.
Only after establishing this connection data will be transmitted.
Datas are splitted in to packets,these packets are transmitted to receiving device. The three way handshake consist of a SYN packet that is sent from the senter computer to the receiving computer.
Then the receiving computer sends a SYN-ACK acknowldgement packet to the sender machine,then it will responds with an ACK packet to the receiving computer.
Then only the connection is established and data is transmitted.

UDP Protocol

Its a protocol that is mainly used for transferring data at a higher rate.
UDP is used in video streaming for continuos transfer of data.
Its a connectionless protocol,that is before transmitting data no conection is established
UDP will not check whether the transmitted data is recieved at the client side ,its the main disadvantage of UDP.
Once UDP start sending data it will not stop until the packets are over.Its used in connections where speed is required than stability of data.
